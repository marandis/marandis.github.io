5 most useful products for women's health

What are the most useful foods for women's health? Do women really need to eat different foods than men? Many people think so. Women's nutritional needs in many ways they differ from the needs of men due to different levels of activity, age, physiology and individual characteristics.

Here are the 5 most useful products for women.

1. Whole-grain products.

Whole grains are rich in fiber and promote proper functioning digestive system. They are very useful for women who have digestive problems, gastritis or colitis. Some of the recommended foods are brown rice and wholegrain bread.

2. Nuts.

Nuts are an excellent source of monounsaturated fats, which help lower cholesterol levels. They they contain protein, calcium, phosphorus, zinc, selenium, vitamin A and vitamin E, which makes them very useful and nutritious. Of course, you need to control the amount, because they contain a lot of calories, and if you overeat, then these calories settle in the body. The ideal daily dose includes:

- 28 peanuts;

- 14 Brazilian dollars orikhiv.

- 7 cashews.

3. Bananas.

One of the eternal battles in which women participate is the fight against fat, and the best weapon in it is to build muscle mass. Bananas are very useful and suitable for this purpose. Because they are rich in potassium, they promote muscle building and recovery fluid lost during sweating. A medium-sized banana contains the same amount of carbohydrates as 2 slices of bread (but still don't eat too much of either of the two foods).

4. Soy protein.

Soy protein can be found in foods such as tofu, soy milk, soy oil, and cereals. The minimum amount of this superfood is 25 g per day. Among the many benefits of soy is its ability to reduce bad cholesterol and increase phytonutrient intake.

5. Eggs.

Today, there are many contradictory myths about consuming an increased amount of eggs. Forget about that, that eating eggs increases the level of bad cholesterol. One egg contains 215 mg of cholesterol, and the required daily dose is about 300 mg. As for the yolk, it is an excellent source of iron and lecithin.

The above products must be included in the daily menu, and a varied diet - a staunch ally of good female health.
