The most important health products for women 50-60 years old

In the fifth decade, women usually begin menopause. There is a serious deficiency of the hormone oestrogen in the body, which leads to a sharp increase in weight. How to balance these changes in the in the body with the help of nutrition? It is necessary to eat several times a day in small quantities, but fasting is not recommended. More plant-based foods in the menu will help provide the body with the necessary vitamins and fiber. Fish, especially sea fish, is necessary at least once or twice a week - the substances contained in it strengthen the immune system they help to improve the cardiovascular system, reduce cholesterol levels, and provide essential fatty acids to compensate for sex hormones. Meat should be on the table no more than once a day, it should be low-fat, it is best to cook it in stewed form. The necessary carbohydrates are best taken in the form of muesli and porridge, and protein - from legumes. You should limit the use of salt, because it increases blood pressure and causes fluid retention, and sugar should be avoided because of cholesterol.

After 60 years.

After 60 years of age, women begin to have problems with the musculoskeletal system, and they become overweight other diseases of the cardiovascular system caused by the deposition of cholesterol on the walls of blood vessels, as well as problems with memory, vision, reduced attention, changes in the vestibular apparatus. Therefore, walking and light exercise are recommended, but regular exercise improves the functioning of the brain and vestibular system the system.

To reduce the level of bad cholesterol in the blood, it is good to include mainly plant-based foods in the menu - they are rich in vitamins and trace elements, so necessary for the aging body. You should limit foods with a high cholesterol content - fat meat, liver, egg yolk, eat more low-fat dairy products and sea fish. Tea is useful, but not with confectionery, but with honey and raisins.

Proper and balanced nutrition at any age is not so difficult. It is important to know the needs of your body and provide it with a full diet and physical activity.
