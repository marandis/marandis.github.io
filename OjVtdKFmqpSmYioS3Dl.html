6 best food supplements for men's health

1. Probiotics.

Probiotics are important for proper digestion. If you want to have a healthy body, first of all you need to take care of the health of the intestines. Related to this some of the main problems that most men experience after the age of 50. Of course, you can get some probiotics, for example, from yogurt, but they can not be compared with a high-quality probiotic Supplement.

2. Arginine, ornithine and glutamine.

If you accept them directly before bedtime, these three key amino acids can help support the natural production of growth hormone, which levels fall with age. Growth hormone released by the pituitary gland is important for tissue repair in the human body. In addition, it is directly associated with normal levels of the male hormone, which is responsible for full sleep and adequate recovery from stress.

3. CoQ10.

The body produces coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) naturally, but its levels decrease with age. Coenzyme Q10 is an important nutrient for cellular energy, and there is an organ that constantly needs energy - it is the heart. why CoQ10 is one of the most popular supplements for men and women over 50 years old.

4. Glucosamine and chondroitin.

Glucosamine and chondroitin stimulate joint and connective tissue health, which helps maintain normal physical activity. If you want to stay active, these two supplements (which are often found in combination) should be at the top of your list.

6. Alpha-lipoic acid.

Alpha-lipoic acid is a fatty acid acid is an antioxidant that is naturally produced in the body and is found in red meat, liver, brewer's yeast and a number of green plants. Studies show that it helps regenerate other antioxidants including vitamin C, vitamin E, and glutathione, supports heart and liver health, and the metabolism of blood sugar.
